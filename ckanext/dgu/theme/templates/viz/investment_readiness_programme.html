    View the code to see how we used the CKAN API to fetch and process the data for this visualisation.

    Investment Contract Readiness Fund (ICRF)

    This visualisation shows investments made by the Investment and Contract Readiness Fund into high growth potential social ventures. The Investment and Contract Readiness Fund pays for business support to help social ventures raise investment of over £500k or win bids to deliver public services contracts valued at over £1m. Hover your mouse over the lines below to view links to the ventures supported and the grant amount received.

    The data is available for the ICRF investments. The data shows: the venture supported, the grant amount, and the postcode where the venture is headquartered.
